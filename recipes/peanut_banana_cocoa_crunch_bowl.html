Peanut Banana Cocoa Crunch

A protein-bowl as an alternative to cereal.

Ingredients:

  • 150g nonfat Greek Yogurt
  • 65g Frozen Banana
  • 1/2 Crumbled Protein Bar of Choice
  • 14g Peanuts
  • 6g Cocoa Nibs
  • Drizzle of Honey

Steps

  1. Layer all components in the listed order to a serving bowl. Adjust amounts to preference.
